Why You Need To Use WeddingIt?

No Upload Limit

WeddingIt allows unlimited uploads from all your guests. All photos and videos are sent to your Google Drive and you have a free 15GB from Google with the option to add more.

Free For Always

Free for you, free for your guests! WeddingIt connects a unique web URL to your Google Drive so all photos and videos are sent directly to you.

Don't Worry about asking your guests to download an app and enter your special key. WeddingIt transfers photos and videos using any web browser.
